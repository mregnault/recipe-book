# Potato-leak soup recipe


## Ingredients

- **some** potatoes, pealed, cleaned and diced
- **1 or 2** leak(s), diced
- (optional) **1 tablespoon** olive oil or salted/unsalted butter
- (optional) **2** garlic cloves, minced
- **Salt and black pepper** to taste


## Instructions

0. **If you add oil or butter**
   - In a large pot, heat the olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add the
     leak dices and cook for about 5 minutes until they are soft.

1. **Put everything to a boil**
   - Add the rest of the ingredients to the pot and cover with water. Bring
     the mixture to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer
     for about 20 minutes.

2. **Blend the Soup**
   - For a smoother soup, use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly
     in the pot to your desired consistency. Alternatively, you can blend the
     soup in batches using a regular blender. Be careful when blending hot
     liquids and allow steam to escape.

3. **Serve:**
   - Ladle the soup into bowls. Serve with crusty bread on the side for dipping.
